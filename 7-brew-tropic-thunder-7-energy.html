How to Make a Copycat 7 Brew Tropic Thunder 7 Energy at Home

Recreate the signature taste of Tropic Thunder 7 Energy in your own kitchen with this simple barista copycat recipe.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz 7 Energy Drink Base
  • 1/2 oz Mango Syrup
  • 1/2 oz Pineapple Syrup
  • Ice

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Add mango and pineapple syrups to the bottom of the glass.
  2. Add ice to the top of the glass.
  3. Pour 7 Energy drink base over the ice and stir well.
