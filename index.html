Frequently Asked Questions

Get quick answers about 7 Brew drinks, customizations, and recipes.

What are the Original 7 drinks at 7 Brew?

The Original 7 signature drinks are the core of the 7 Brew menu:

  • Blondie: Caramel and Vanilla Breve
  • Brunette: Hazelnut and Caramel Mocha
  • Smooth 7: White Chocolate and Irish Cream Breve
  • White Mac: White Chocolate and Macadamia Nut Breve
  • German Chocolate: Coconut and Caramel Mocha
  • Snickerz: Hazelnut and Caramel Mocha with Caramel Drizzle
  • Cinnamon Roll: Brown Sugar Cinnamon and White Chocolate Breve

Can I get sugar-free drinks at 7 Brew?

Absolutely! 7 Brew offers an extensive selection of sugar-free syrups (like Vanilla, Caramel, Irish Cream, White Chocolate, Coconut, and Raspberry) that can be mixed into any coffee, rebel energy drink, or tea. Check out our Calorie Calculator to customize low-calorie options.

What is a 7 Energy drink?

7 Energy is 7 Brew's exclusive premium energy drink base. You can order it sugar-free or regular and customize it with any fruit syrups (such as Blue Raspberry, Strawberry, Kiwi, Passion Fruit, and Peach) to make popular custom mixes like the Love Potion or Pixie Stick.

How do I make 7 Brew copycat drinks at home?

You can recreate signature 7 Brew drinks by using espresso, half-and-half (for breves), and Torani syrups in the correct ratios (typically 1 to 2 scoops of flavor syrup). Visit our interactive Recipe Maker to get direct step-by-step copycat recipes and ratios!
