Rewards FAQs

How do I sign up for the rewards program? ▾

You can sign up in less than 30 seconds at any 7 Brew stand by giving your phone number to the Brewista, or by visiting the official corporate website. There is no app to download.

Do I need to show my ID for my birthday drink? ▾

No! Once your birthday is registered in your loyalty account, the free drink credit is loaded automatically and connected directly to your phone number in the system.

What should I do if I am missing points? ▾

If points from a recent transaction didn't register on your account, visit the support page on the official 7 Brew website and submit a support ticket including your phone number and stand location.
