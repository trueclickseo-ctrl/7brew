Seven Energy Blue Punch

Custom Drive-Thru Flavor

The 7 Brew Seven Energy Blue Punch is a stellar representative of our 7 Energy line. Expertly crafted by local Brewistas, this custom mix balances sweet syrups with a rich coffee or fruit base. Indulge in its unique flavor profile, whether served hot, over ice, or blended as a frozen chiller.

What is a 7 Brew Seven Energy Blue Punch?

The 7 Brew Seven Energy Blue Punch is a highly customizable drink popular at our drive-thru locations. It features a bold blend of premium ingredients: 7 Brew Energy Base, Blue Raspberry Syrup, Peach Syrup, Raspberry Syrup. By marrying these complex flavors together, our baristas create a sweet and satisfying profile that caters to coffee enthusiasts and casual drink fans alike.

Ingredients & Flavor Profile

The flavor profile of the Seven Energy Blue Punch is defined by the high-quality syrups and base components used in its assembly. Each ingredient plays a vital role. For coffee-based versions, the bold roast of our espresso balances out the sweet caramel, chocolate, or fruit flavors. For energy or tea mixes, the effervescent carbonation or organic tea notes create a clean and crisp background. It is a harmonious combination designed to spark your taste buds.

How to Order the Seven Energy Blue Punch

When ordering at the stand, simply ask your Brewista for a "Seven Energy Blue Punch" in your preferred size: Small (16 oz), Medium (24 oz), or Large (32 oz). You can specify whether you want it hot, iced, or blended as a frozen chiller. To adjust the sweetness, you can ask for it "half-sweet" or request extra shots of espresso to cut the syrup richness.

Customization: Dairy-Free & Sugar-Free Options

Staying on track with dietary goals is easy at 7 Brew. You can request a Sugar-Free version of the Seven Energy Blue Punch using our sugar-free syrup library. Additionally, you can swap out the default milk base (like heavy half-and-half or whole milk) for plant-based alternatives like Oat Milk, Coconut Milk, or Almond Milk. This reduces calorie density while keeping a smooth, velvety texture.

Why Customers Love It

Customers rave about the Seven Energy Blue Punch because it is the perfect representation of what makes 7 Brew great: bold flavor, rapid speed, and incredible customizability. It acts as a great pick-me-up during morning commutes or as an afternoon sweet treat.

How to Make a Copycat 7 Brew Seven Energy Blue Punch at Home

Can't make it to a drive-thru stand? Here is a basic copycat barista formula to mix your own custom version of the Seven Energy Blue Punch in your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 oz Torani 7 Brew Energy Base
  • 1/2 oz Torani Blue Raspberry Syrup
  • 1/2 oz Torani Peach Syrup
  • 1/2 oz Torani Raspberry Syrup
  • 1 cup of ice
  • Water/Milk option depending on preference

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Mix Syrups: Combine flavor syrups in your glass.
  2. Add Espresso/Base: Pour freshly brewed hot espresso shots or your cold base drink over the syrups and stir.
  3. Add Ice & Milk: Fill with ice, top off with milk, and stir gently to combine.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get a sugar-free 7 Brew Seven Energy Blue Punch?

Yes! You can request sugar-free syrups for any of the flavors in this drink. We also offer sugar-free milk alternatives to help keep sugar counts to a minimum.

What base is used for the Seven Energy Blue Punch?

The base beverage varies by category. For breves, it is rich espresso and half-and-half. For energy drinks, it uses our proprietary Seven Energy base.
