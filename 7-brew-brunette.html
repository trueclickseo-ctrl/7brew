How to Make a Copycat 7 Brew The Brunette at Home

Recreate the signature taste of The Brunette in your own kitchen with this simple barista copycat recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 shots of espresso
  • 1/2 oz Hazelnut Syrup
  • 1/2 oz Caramel Syrup
  • 6 oz Chocolate Milk
  • Ice

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Add hazelnut and caramel syrups to a tall glass.
  2. Brew 2 fresh espresso shots directly over the syrups and stir.
  3. Fill with ice and pour chocolate milk over the top. Stir well.
