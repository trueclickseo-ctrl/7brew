Frequently Asked Questions

What is the 7 Originals line? ▾

The 7 Originals are our signature line of breve and mocha beverages: Blondie, Brunette, Smooth 7, Cinnamon Roll, White Mac, German Chocolate, and Triple Seven. Each offers a unique blend of syrups and sauces.

Do you offer sugar-free alternatives? ▾

Yes, absolutely! We offer sugar-free options for almost all of our signature syrups, including sugar-free vanilla, caramel, white chocolate, and more. Use our Calorie Calculator to view adjusted figures.

What are the operating hours? ▾

Most of our locations open at 5:30 AM and close at 10:00 PM on weekdays, and 11:00 PM on Friday and Saturday nights. Check the Locations finder to confirm details for your closest stand.

Can I open a franchise location? ▾

Yes! We are always looking for passionate partners to expand our high-energy coffee community. Please select "Franchise Opportunities" in the contact form to connect with our development leads.
