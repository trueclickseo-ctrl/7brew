How to Make a Copycat 7 Brew Sunrise 7 Energy at Home

Recreate the signature taste of Sunrise 7 Energy in your own kitchen with this simple barista copycat recipe.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz 7 Energy Drink Base
  • 1/2 oz Peach Syrup
  • 1/2 oz Raspberry Syrup
  • Ice

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Pump peach and raspberry syrups into the glass.
  2. Fill the glass to the top with ice.
  3. Pour 7 Energy base over the ice and stir thoroughly.
