How to Make a Copycat 7 Brew Triple Seven at Home

Barista copycat recipe to make a high-caffeine iced Triple Seven breve at home.

Ingredients

  • 3 shots of fresh espresso
  • 1/2 oz Torani Caramel Syrup
  • 1/2 oz Torani Vanilla Syrup
  • 6 oz Half-and-Half (breve milk)
  • 1 cup of ice

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Syrup Base: Pump 1/2 oz of caramel syrup and 1/2 oz of vanilla syrup in a cup.
  2. Espresso: Brew 3 fresh espresso shots directly over the syrups and stir.
  3. Milk & Ice: Add ice cubes to the top, pour 6 oz of half-and-half over the ice, and stir to incorporate.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much caffeine is in a Triple Seven?

A Triple Seven breve contains approximately 225 mg of caffeine because it is brewed with 3 shots of espresso.

Is it possible to customize the milk type?

Yes! You can ask for oat milk, almond milk, or coconut milk instead of the standard half-and-half to fit your dietary needs.
