How to Make a Copycat 7 Brew Snickerz at Home

Recreate the signature taste of Snickerz in your own kitchen with this simple barista copycat recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 shots of espresso
  • 1/2 oz Hazelnut Syrup
  • 1/2 oz Caramel Syrup
  • 6 oz Chocolate Milk
  • Caramel Sauce Drizzle
  • Ice

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Combine hazelnut and caramel syrups in the cup.
  2. Brew 2 fresh espresso shots and pour over, stirring well.
  3. Fill with ice, pour chocolate milk, and finish with a rich caramel drizzle.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the 7 Brew Snickerz sweet?

Yes, it is a dessert-style mocha with caramel drizzle, hazelnut, and chocolate.

How do I order Snickerz sugar-free?

Ask for sugar-free hazelnut, sugar-free caramel, skim milk, and sugar-free chocolate drizzle.
