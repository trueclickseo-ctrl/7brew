Financial Requirements

Liquid Capital Requirements

A minimum of $300,000 in liquid assets (cash, stocks, or other easily convertible funds) is required per agreement.

Net Worth Minimum

A minimum personal or group net worth of $1,000,000 is required to qualify for site financing and development leases.

Total Initial Investment

The total estimated setup cost for a dual-lane prefab modular drive-thru stand ranges between $887,000 and $1,950,000, including the $35,000-$45,000 franchise fee.

Franchise FAQ

What is the franchise fee for a 7 Brew stand? ▾

The standard franchise fee is between $35,000 and $45,000 per stand location. This covers the license to operate, brand training, and corporate development support.

What is the average size of a stand? ▾

7 Brew stands are prefabricated modular buildings, typically around 500 square feet, featuring dual-lane drive-thru service with no indoor seating to maximize transaction speeds.

What are the ongoing royalty fees? ▾

Ongoing royalty fees range from 4.5% to 7% of gross monthly sales, plus an additional advertising fee of approximately 2% of sales to fund brand marketing programs.
